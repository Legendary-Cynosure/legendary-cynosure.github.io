About Me

Hi! I’m currently a junior college student planning on transferring to a university in 2023. Maybe UCLA or UC Berkeley, I've toured both on Minecraft and I really admire the faculty and drive at both schools. I am passionate about machine learning and data science, specifically the massive potential to develop technologies at the intersection of human-computer interaction. I am an incoming data science intern at Intel corporation this summer and I’m super excited! Other than that, I recently joined NASA’s NCAS Aerospace Scholar program. Previous experiences include working on a team with IBM to promote digital access and literacy to underserved communities across Africa and Inner Mongolia. I also led ACM at Los Rios as the Vice President in 2022 and founded CityHacks, the college district’s first collegiate hackathon.
